## üì¶ Description

**YAIM0425 d03b ‚Äì Robots with Immunity** adds special versions of construction and logistic robots that are **immune to damage**.

This MOD creates:

* ü§ñ **One robot per damage type**, fully immune to that specific damage
* üßø **One ultimate robot** that is immune to **all damage types**

These robots are designed for extreme environments where normal robots would be constantly destroyed.

---

## ‚öôÔ∏è How it works (Player view)

* The MOD creates **copies** of the vanilla construction and logistic robots
* These robots behave exactly like normal robots
* The only difference is their **damage immunity**, depending on the variant
* The ultimate version cannot be damaged by any source

No configuration is required. Just craft and use them.
